Overcoming Obstacles

Challenges & Solutions

As first-year students with no experience, we faced unique challenges and found innovative solutions

01

Technical Knowledge Gap

Starting with zero knowledge of EV systems, automotive engineering, or advanced CAD design.

Our Solution:

Intensive self-learning through online courses, peer-to-peer teaching sessions, and guidance from senior mentors.

02

Limited Resources

Limited access to advanced fabrication tools, testing equipment, and industry-grade components.

Our Solution:

Creative problem-solving using college workshop facilities, DIY approaches, and collaboration with local workshops.

03

Time Management

Balancing rigorous first-year academic schedule with demanding EV project requirements.

Our Solution:

Structured weekly planning, efficient task delegation, and dedicated weekend work sessions.

04

Team Coordination

First experience working in a large multidisciplinary team on a complex engineering project.

Our Solution:

Regular team meetings, clear communication protocols, and collaborative decision-making processes.

05

Testing Failures

Multiple component failures and design flaws during initial prototyping and testing phases.

Our Solution:

Adopting a fail-fast, learn-quickly approach with systematic documentation of every failure for continuous improvement.

06

Academic Pressure

Managing first-year coursework, exams, and project deadlines simultaneously.

Our Solution:

Integrating project work with academic learning, seeking project-relevant coursework, and maintaining study-work balance.
