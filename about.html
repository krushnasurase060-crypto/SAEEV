Our Origin

The Vajrablaze Story

How a group of passionate engineering students came together to revolutionize electric mobility

2023

The Inception

Founded by a group of visionary engineering students from Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering who shared a common passion for sustainable transportation and electric vehicle technology.

2024

Team Formation

Expanded to include students from F.Y., S.Y., and T.Y. engineering programs, creating a multidisciplinary team with diverse skills in mechanical, electrical, electronics, and computer engineering.

Present

Competition Preparation

Currently designing and building an electric scooter from scratch for the prestigious EV scooter competition, focusing on innovation, efficiency, and performance.
