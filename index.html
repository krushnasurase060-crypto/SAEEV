Our EV Scooter Innovation

Explore the advanced components that power our high-performance electric scooters

Battery System

72V 30Ah lithium-ion battery with advanced thermal management and fast charging capability.

Electric Motor

5kW brushless DC motor with 95% efficiency and regenerative braking system.

Controller Unit

Smart controller with IoT connectivity and real-time performance monitoring.

BMS

Advanced battery management system with cell balancing and safety protection.

Digital Dashboard

7-inch touch display with navigation, diagnostics, and customizable interface.

Lightweight Frame

Carbon fiber composite frame providing strength while minimizing weight.
